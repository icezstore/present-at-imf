Ali Shareef Al Emadi, Qatar’s Minister of Finance present at IMF and World Bank’s joint meeting in Washington

October 30, 2019October 30, 2019| Sharon GrantSharon Grant| 0 Comment

During the conference, the Committee addressed ways that will further improve economic and financial cooperation among members of the GCC and increase profits. Ali Shareef Al Emadi, Finance Minister Ali Shareef Al Emadi, met with the Prime Minister of the Federal Republic of Somalia Hassan Khairi on the sidelines of Qatar’s participation in the IMF and World Bank Group annual autumn meetings in Washington last October 14-20. 

The Finance Minister also met with the US Secretary of the Treasury, Steven Mnuchin, the Sudanese Minister of Finance, Dr. Ibrahim Al Badawi, and a host of ministers who participated in the meetings and heads of bank boards.

The Qatari delegation was headed by Ali Shareef Al Emadi and held a number of meetings with senior officials from the IMF and the World Bank Group, together with bilateral meetings with delegations from other countries. The attending delegation participated in a number of deliberative meetings to discuss financial policies, new developments and the economic challenges facing the world at the local and international level.

The gathering in autumn brings together thousands of political leaders, the private sector and scholars from around the world to discuss issues of global concern, including the economic outlook, poverty reduction, economic development, and aid sustainability. Also, during these meetings regional conferences, seminars, and many other activities and events focused on the global economy, international development and global financial markets took place.
